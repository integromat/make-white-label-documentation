# Requirements

Make's Infra/CloudOps configures your Make White Label instance for a custom domain. However, you and your company are responsible for the following:

1. Purchase and maintain your own custom domain.

{% hint style="warning" %}
You are responsible for all costs, actions, and maintenance related to acquiring and maintaining your custom domain.
{% endhint %}

2. Manage specific DNS NS records for your domain.
3. Decide on an OAuth redirect URI for apps on your instance.

{% hint style="info" %}
Begin your OAuth migration before we complete your custom domain. See the details in the [OAuth redirect section](app-oauth-redirect-uris.md).
{% endhint %}

4. Reconfigure SSO redirect URI.
